## SAIOS in One Sentence

SAIOS (Stoke-AI Operating System) is a team of five AI agents that runs your business operations 24/7 — handling client communication, lead follow-up, document management, market research, and content creation — so you can focus on the work that actually earns money.

## What It Is (And What It Isn't)

**SAIOS is:**
- An operating system — like iOS runs your phone, SAIOS runs your business
- A team of 5 AI agents that coordinate with each other
- Configured specifically for YOUR business, YOUR clients, YOUR market
- A system that learns and improves over time
- Running 24/7 on dedicated hardware (a Mac mini in our office)

**SAIOS is not:**
- A chatbot (though it can chat)
- A single AI tool (it's an integrated system)
- A generic software product (each deployment is custom)
- A replacement for you (it handles operations so you handle relationships)
- Cloud-based SaaS (your data stays on dedicated hardware)

## The Five Agents

### 1. Main Agent — The Coordinator
Your central hub. Triages all incoming communication (email, text, chat), delegates tasks to other agents, and ensures nothing falls through the cracks. Think of it as a chief of staff who works 24/7.

### 2. Doc Agent — The Paper Handler
Manages all documents, contracts, and files. Preps meeting materials before appointments. Drafts proposals and summaries. Keeps everything organized and findable. Never loses a file.

### 3. Scout Agent — The Market Watcher
Monitors your market, tracks competitors, and surfaces opportunities. For insurance agents: rate changes, carrier updates, regulatory shifts. For realtors: new listings, market trends, comparable sales. For any business: competitive intelligence that arrives before you ask for it.

### 4. Builder Agent — The Tech Team
Handles technical operations: system integrations, website updates, workflow automation, and data management. You never touch the backend — Builder handles it.

### 5. Content Agent — The Voice
Creates all client-facing content: follow-up emails, newsletters, social media posts, marketing materials. Writes in your voice, based on your actual communication style. Not generic templates — content that sounds like you wrote it.

## How It Works Day-to-Day

**6:00 AM:** A client texts about their policy renewal. Main Agent reads it, checks the context (Doc Agent pulls the policy details), and sends an informed response. You're still asleep.

**8:00 AM:** You check your morning dashboard. Three things need your attention today. Everything else is handled. You see that SAIOS responded to 7 inquiries overnight, booked 2 appointments, and sent 12 follow-ups.

**10:00 AM:** You have a client meeting. Doc Agent has already prepared a summary of their account, flagged two coverage gaps, and drafted talking points. You walk in prepared without spending 30 minutes on prep.

**2:00 PM:** A new lead fills out your website form. Main Agent qualifies them in real-time, answers their initial questions, and books an appointment on your calendar for tomorrow. You didn't even know the lead existed until it appeared on your schedule.

**6:00 PM:** You're done for the day. SAIOS keeps working — handling texts, responding to inquiries, sending follow-ups, updating records.

**Result:** You worked 8 hours on client relationships and business development. SAIOS worked 24 hours on everything else.
